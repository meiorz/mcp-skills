# ContentFactory

## Overview

AI-powered media brand builder that turns a topic into research, scripts, audio, video, live streams, distribution, and archives.

## Core Flow

1. Research the topic from papers and OSINT sources.
2. Shape the episode brand kit, thumbnails, and script.
3. Generate audio and video assets, then publish them to the web and stream surfaces.
4. Archive the output and convert each episode into long-form documentation.
