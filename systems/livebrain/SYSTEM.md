# LiveBrain

## Overview

Always-on intelligence aggregation system for weather, threats, papers, and market signals. It ingests heterogeneous sources continuously and exposes briefings, alerts, and dashboards on demand.

## Core Flow

1. Ingest multiple external feeds on a schedule.
2. Merge entities and relationships into a shared knowledge layer.
3. Serve briefings through speech, documentation, messaging, and visualization surfaces.
